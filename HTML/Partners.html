Partnership & Collaboration

Ethio-Academy is committed to building strong partnerships and collaborations with institutions, organizations, and individuals who share our vision of enhancing education and promoting lifelong learning in Ethiopia and beyond.

Why Partner with Ethio-Academy?

  • Access to a diverse range of high-quality e-learning courses.
  • Opportunities to contribute to the development of educational content.
  • Expand your reach and impact by leveraging our platform.
  • Engage with a community of learners and educators passionate about continuous learning.

Collaboration Opportunities

We welcome collaboration in various forms, including but not limited to:

  • Content partnerships: Collaborate on developing and delivering online courses.
  • Research collaborations: Conduct joint research projects in relevant fields.
  • Technology partnerships: Explore opportunities to enhance our e-learning platform.
  • Outreach and advocacy: Partner on initiatives to promote education and lifelong learning.

Contact Us

If you are interested in exploring partnership or collaboration opportunities with Ethio-Academy, please contact us at partnerships@ethioacademy.com.
