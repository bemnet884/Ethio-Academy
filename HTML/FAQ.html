Is the website free to use?

Yes, the website is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, there are no hidden costs associated with using the website. All courses and resources are available free of charge.

Do I need to create an account to access courses?

No, creating an account is optional. You can access all courses and resources without creating an account.

Can I share the courses with others?

Yes, you're welcome to share the courses with others. The more people who benefit from our resources, the better!
