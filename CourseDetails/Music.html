Music Theory and Appreciation

Instructor: Prof. Emily Davis

Duration: 8 weeks

Price: $99

Dive into the world of music with our comprehensive course on music theory and appreciation. From the fundamentals of rhythm and melody to the exploration of different musical genres, this course will enhance your understanding and enjoyment of music.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to Music Theory
  • Elements of Music: Pitch, Rhythm, and Harmony
  • Music Notation and Sight-Reading
  • Chords and Scales
  • Musical Forms and Structures
  • Genres of Music: Classical, Jazz, Blues, Rock, etc.
  • Music History and Evolution
  • Listening Skills and Analysis
  • Composition and Arrangement Techniques
  • Performance and Interpretation
  • Final Project: Creating a Musical Portfolio

Requirements:

  • No prior musical experience required
  • Access to internet and a computer/laptop

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive understanding of music theory concepts
  • Appreciation of various musical styles and genres
  • Enhanced listening skills and musical analysis abilities
  • Hands-on experience in composition and arrangement
  • Certificate of completion upon finishing the course
  • Access to additional study materials and resources
