Graphic Design: Fundamentals and Techniques

Instructor: Graphic Designer, Emily Smith

Duration: 8 weeks

Price: $129

Explore the creative world of graphic design with our comprehensive course. From typography to color theory, this course will teach you the essential principles and techniques needed to create stunning visual designs.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to Graphic Design
  • Typography and Fonts
  • Color Theory
  • Layout and Composition
  • Image Editing with Adobe Photoshop
  • Vector Graphics with Adobe Illustrator
  • Logo Design and Branding
  • Print Design Fundamentals
  • Web Design Basics
  • UI/UX Design Principles
  • Final Project: Designing a Brand Identity

Requirements:

  • No prior design experience required
  • Access to graphic design software (Adobe Creative Suite)
  • Access to internet and a computer/laptop

Benefits:

  • Hands-on experience with industry-standard design tools
  • Practical design projects to build your portfolio
  • Feedback and guidance from experienced designers
  • Opportunity to explore various design specialties
  • Certificate of completion upon finishing the course
  • Access to additional design resources and communities
