History: Understanding the Past

Instructor: Prof. Michael Johnson

Duration: 10 weeks

Price: $129

Explore the rich tapestry of human history with our comprehensive course. From ancient civilizations to modern societies, this course will take you on a journey through the key events, figures, and themes that have shaped our world.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to Historical Studies
  • Ancient Civilizations
  • Classical Antiquity
  • Medieval History
  • Renaissance and Reformation
  • Age of Exploration
  • Enlightenment and Revolution
  • Industrialization and Imperialism
  • World Wars and Global Conflict
  • Cold War Era
  • Modern Times
  • Final Project: Analyzing Historical Events

Requirements:

  • Interest in history and world affairs
  • Basic research and critical thinking skills
  • Access to internet and a computer/laptop

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive overview of key historical periods
  • Insightful analysis of historical events and their impact on society
  • Engaging lectures, readings, and multimedia resources
  • Opportunity for discussion and debate with peers
  • Certificate of completion upon finishing the course
  • Access to additional study materials and resources
