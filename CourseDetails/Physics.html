Physics: Understanding the Universe

Instructor: Prof. David Williams

Duration: 10 weeks

Price: $139

Dive into the fundamental principles of physics with our engaging course. From classical mechanics to quantum theory, this course will unravel the mysteries of the universe and its natural phenomena.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to Physics
  • Mechanics: Kinematics and Dynamics
  • Thermodynamics and Statistical Mechanics
  • Electromagnetism
  • Optics and Wave Phenomena
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Relativity: Special and General
  • Nuclear Physics
  • Particle Physics
  • Astrophysics and Cosmology
  • Experimental Methods in Physics
  • Final Project: Exploring a Physics Phenomenon

Requirements:

  • No prior knowledge of physics required
  • Basic understanding of mathematics (recommended)
  • Access to internet and a computer/laptop

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive coverage of key physics principles
  • Insights into the workings of the natural world
  • Enhanced problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Opportunity to explore cutting-edge research areas
  • Certificate of completion upon finishing the course
  • Access to additional study materials and resources
