Geography: Exploring the World

Instructor: Prof. Michael Johnson

Duration: 10 weeks

Price: $129

Embark on a journey around the globe with our comprehensive course on geography. From the Earth's physical features to human civilizations and cultures, this course will deepen your understanding of the world we live in.

Course Content:

  • Introduction to Geography: Concepts and Themes
  • Earth's Physical Features: Landforms, Oceans, and Atmosphere
  • Weather and Climate
  • Biomes and Ecosystems
  • Population Geography
  • Cultural Geography: Languages, Religions, and Traditions
  • Political Geography: Nations, States, and Borders
  • Economic Geography: Resources, Trade, and Development
  • Urban Geography: Cities and Megacities
  • Environmental Issues and Sustainability
  • Geographic Information Systems (GIS)
  • Final Project: Exploring a Geographic Region

Requirements:

  • No prior knowledge of geography required
  • Access to internet and a computer/laptop

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive understanding of global geography
  • Insights into environmental and societal issues
  • Enhanced spatial thinking and analytical skills
  • Opportunity to explore diverse cultures and landscapes
  • Certificate of completion upon finishing the course
  • Access to additional study materials and resources
